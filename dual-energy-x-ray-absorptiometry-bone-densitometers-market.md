<p><p>The Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DXA) bone densitometer market is segmented into axial and peripheral devices. Axial bone densitometers assess bone density in central skeletal regions, such as the spine and hip, providing comprehensive diagnostic information. In contrast, peripheral bone densitometers measure bone density in extremities like the wrist and heel, offering a more portable and less expensive alternative, often used for screening. Both types are essential for diagnosing osteoporosis and other bone-related conditions.</p></p>
