<p><strong>The Ventilation Masks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive Ventilation Masks</li><li>Non-invasive Ventilation Masks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilation masks market includes two primary types: intrusive and non-invasive ventilation masks. Intrusive ventilation masks, typically used in more severe respiratory conditions, require intubation and are designed for direct airway access. In contrast, non-invasive ventilation masks, which cover the nose or mouth, provide therapeutic support without the need for invasive procedures, making them suitable for patients with less severe respiratory issues. The choice between them depends on the patient's condition and the required level of respiratory support.</p></p>
